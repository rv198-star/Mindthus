Keep each piece of intake evidence linked to its recording:

- Folded language note: leave with the tape case, photograph/scan it, and record the language note in intake metadata.
- String label: keep it attached if it is not damaging the reel; otherwise remove carefully, store with the reel, and transcribe/photograph it.
- Voicemail about rights: save the voicemail if possible, transcribe or summarize it, record who left it and when, and mark the rights status as source-based/provisional.
- Unnamed new reel: assign a temporary identifier such as `TEMP-2026-07-13-001`, attach it to the reel/enclosure, and use that same ID in the intake log until formal cataloguing.
