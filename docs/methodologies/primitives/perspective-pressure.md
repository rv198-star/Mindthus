# Perspective Pressure / 视角施压

## Role

Perspective Pressure challenges a conclusion that has become too clean inside one
viewpoint. It is useful when role, incentive, or stakeholder position may change what
counts as a good judgment.

## Short Rule

单一视角过度自洽时，用角色压力或激励检查挑战判断。

## When It Intervenes

- In `SELA`, when system efficiency framing may erase local value, timing, or hard
  boundaries.
- In `EDSP`, when the coordinate system looks elegant but may be over-built from one
  side.
- At freeze or continue points where a hidden incentive could change the answer.

## Action Effects

- Add a challenger role.
- Rebuild a coordinate or boundary.
- Downgrade a claim.
- Block a conclusion until the conflicting perspective is handled.

## Must Not

- It must not become debate theater.
- It must not replace facts or stakeholder authority.
- It must not be applied universally to every task.

## Probe

Whose position makes this conclusion look weaker, costlier, riskier, or less useful?
