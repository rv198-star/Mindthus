## Boundary

- The external cinematic skill remains a behavior sample, not source truth.
- This log may summarize donor-skill behavior families, but it should not store copied
  donor prompt wording as reusable package content.
- Images2 output remains loop-assisted production evidence for one run, not proof that
  the profile is generally strong.

strengthening profile surfaces rather than trying to clone donor phrasing.

## 2026-07-02 to 2026-07-03 - Han Li / Dayan Sword Formation

Subject: `韩立释放大衍剑阵`

Observed sequence:

- high pressure could create stronger field force while also introducing too many equal
  action beats

  battlefield into a smaller-feeling scene

  debris, and sword streaks into reaction, aftermath, or directional support

Profile surfaces extracted from this sequence:

- `shot economy mode`
- `director subtraction pass`
