# WAE Fidelity Contract

This Fidelity Contract defines required judgment moves for WAE.

required judgment moves:

- identify whether uncertainty is mainly path, truth, evidence, authority, or risk
- decide which layer should control: workflow, agentic judgment, evidence, or human
  escalation
- apply reversibility, blast radius, tool tier, and invocation context risk modulators
- add evidence bridges where claims need proof or confidence caps
- prevent schemas, worksheets, or clean structure from replacing judgment

Allowed exits:

- `not_applicable`: the work is deterministic, low-risk, and already mechanically
  controlled.
- `transfer`: another Mindthus method owns the active semantic judgment.
- `challenge premise`: the workflow or schema is trying to freeze uncertain truth.

shape pass is not semantic approval. scripts must not decide semantic truth; WAE
protects control boundaries rather than filling a worksheet for its own sake.
