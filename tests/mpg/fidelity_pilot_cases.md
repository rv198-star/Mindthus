# MPG Fidelity Pilot

Purpose: validate that Method Fidelity Harness transfers beyond SELA.

Scoring rule: MPG replay is not outcome hit rate. It checks whether the run separated
mainline, path, carrier, vehicle, exposure, optionality, triggers, and challenge
conditions under the available information slice.

This file includes at least one non-stock case.

## Cases

### Case 1: AI equity drawdown

Mainline claim: AI infrastructure will keep expanding.

Carrier question: can this investor hold a specific AI equity through multiple 70%
drawdowns without forced selling?

Expected faithful moves:

- constrain the mainline by horizon and valuation
- separate AI adoption from one stock or one entry price
- map rate, capex, regulation, earnings, and liquidity counter-forces
- define exposure budget and trigger conditions
- keep AQM visibility layer from becoming a price target

### Case 2: company transformation

Mainline claim: a manufacturing company must become software-enabled.

Carrier question: can the current team, cashflow, and customer base carry the
transformation path?

Expected faithful moves:

- separate transformation direction from current organizational vehicle
- map legacy-customer resistance, cash burn, capability gaps, and board patience
- design optionality through pilot business lines or staged hiring
- challenge the mainline if the constrained horizon makes survival unlikely

### Case 3: central-local implementation

Mainline claim: central policy direction favors a new industrial upgrade.

Carrier question: can a local government or operating unit implement it without local
incentives bending the path?

Expected faithful moves:

- distinguish central mainline from local execution vehicle
- map fiscal pressure, employment incentives, reporting incentives, and local champions
- define triggers that reveal real implementation versus slogan compliance
- use MPG instead of treating counter-forces as villains
